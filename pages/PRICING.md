## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try before I buy?

The free version includes all core PDF viewing and SEO features. Try it out, and upgrade when you need premium features.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfer for agency plans.

### Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! Contact support to upgrade. You'll only pay the difference for the remaining license period.

### What happens when my license expires?

The plugin continues to work, but you won't receive updates or support. Renew anytime to get the latest features and security updates.

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If the plugin doesn't meet your needs, we'll refund your purchase.

### Is there a discount for non-profits?

Yes! Contact us for special pricing for non-profits and educational institutions.

### Can I use one license on localhost and production?

Yes, development and staging environments are free. Only production sites count toward your limit.
